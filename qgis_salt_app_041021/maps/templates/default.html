INTRODUCTION

Several studies suggest that we need to significantly increase the world's food production by 2050. However, saltwater intrusion, which has increased due to climate change, is decreasing farm productivity and production in farmlands. Therefore, mapping soil salinity is vital for analyzing trends in soil salinity, understanding root causes, and developing salinity mitigation solutions.

Using remote-sensing modeling, we have produced a salinity map of different counties which display the land type for each coobrdinate. This will enable farmers to find out if there is a salt crust in their farmland which needs to be taken care of to maximize food yield.

In this app, we have remote-sensing modeling to map soil salinity. Just enter your location (lat,long) manually, or allow the app to use your current location and you will see the land type in your location for different years to see how it has changed and if there has ever been a salt crust there.
